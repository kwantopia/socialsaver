- How to Use the Mobile App -

  • Where can I get the mobile application?
  • The MealTime mobile application is available through the Apple App Store for the iPhone and iPod Touch, or through the Android Market for Android phones.
  • The Android App:

    • See recent TechCash transactions.
    Click a transaction to review.
    The MealTime Menu
    See a list of TechCash-accepting venues.
    Click a venue to see reviews.
    You may also see friends that have visited the venue.
    Click a friend to see transactions, reviews, and contact information.
  • The iPhone App:

    • View your recent purchases.
    Check statistics of your purchase histories.
    Click a transaction to review.
    View friends who have visited the same location.
    Click a friend to see transaction histories.
