    What browsers are supported by the Shoppley website?
    We recommend viewing this site on Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Safari, or Internet Explorer 8. Earlier browser versions may not be supported.
    Where can I get the mobile application?
    The OTN mobile application is available through the Apple App Store for the iPhone and iPod Touch, or through the Android Market for Android phones.
    How is Facebook Connect used?
    Facebook Connect is used so that you do not have to remember or maintain a separate user name or password. When you login with Facebook Connect, we may know the friend relationship between your account and existing accounts or new accounts created by your friends (feature provided by Facebook). The data is only collected for research purposes and will be either hashed or purged once the research is over upon user's request.
    How can I contact the researchers?
    Currently Kwan Lee, Yod Watanaprakornkul, Eric Shyu, and Stephanie Chang from the MIT Media Lab are working on this research. Please feel free to contact kwan at media dot mit dot edu to help us improve and make the service better so larger number of people can participate.
