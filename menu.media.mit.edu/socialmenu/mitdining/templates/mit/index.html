    Your phone is the menu!

    The MIT Media Lab is conducting a digital menu trial at campus dining locations at MIT location. Use your phone to browse the menu! After your visit, log back on to this site and tell us about your meal.

    Experiment Steps:

      {% load facebook_tags %}
    • Step 1: Log in with {% show_connect_button %}
    • Step 2: Download the application to your phone
    • Step 3: Eat campus dining locations and pay with TechCASH or Dining Dollars
    • Step 4: Rate your meal on this site
    • Step 5: Each time you pay with TechCASH/Dining Dollars and rate your meal you are entered to win $100 TechCASH!

    Supported Phones

    • iPhone or iPod Touch
    • Android Phones (Droid, myTouch, etc)
    • Palm WebOS (Pre, Pixi)

    Participating Locations

    You must be a member of the MIT Community and be willing to pay for your items using TechCASH or Dining Dollars
