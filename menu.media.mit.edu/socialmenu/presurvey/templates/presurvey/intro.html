What is this?- Pick A Dish is a short survey from the MIT Media Lab on understanding people's menu choices.

Participate- After you complete the survey, you will be eligible to participate in the digital menu research at Legal Sea Foods. If you are interested in participating, please enter your e-mail address in the survey so we may contact you for the study. You will receive a $10 off your dinner entree.

Invite- Please invite at least 10 of your friends to share their tastes to see how their tastes compare.

The survey should take less than 5 minutes to complete.
