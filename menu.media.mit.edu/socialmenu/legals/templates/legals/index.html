    Your phone is the menu!

    The MIT Media Lab is conducting a digital menu trial at Legal Sea Food's Kendall Square location. Instead of a paper menu, you use your phone. After your visit, log back on to this site and tell us about your meal.

    Experiment Steps:

      {% load facebook_tags %}
    • Step 1: Create PIN by clicking {% show_connect_button %} and logging in. Fill out the presurvey. Register 24 hours before the day of visit. Your name should be on the guest list to receive the $10 discount voucher. Facebook is only used for logging in and tracking friend participation. No data is published to Facebook.
    • Step 2: Visit Legal Sea Food's Kendall Square, Cambridge for DINNER (after 5 PM) and check in with the host indicating you are participating in the Digital Menu trial. Phones from restaurant will be used instead of paper menu to order. You will have to pay for your own meal, but $10 voucher can be used immediately for your entree order.
    • Step 3: After your visit, rate your meal and upload your receipt on this web site and you will receive $10 Amazon gift certificate per visit. If instead you link your buxfer.com account you will receive $30 Amazon gift certificate (see FAQ).
    • Bonus 1: $10 Amazon gift certificate for completing all the surveys.
    • Bonus 2: Come in group of 4 and instead of $10 voucher each, receive a $50 gift card for the group. If you are coming in group of 4 e-mail kwan at media.mit.edu a DAY BEFORE so I may prearrange the gift card.
