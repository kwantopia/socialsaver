CONSENT TO PARTICIPATE IN NON-BIOMEDICAL RESEARCH

Understanding People’s Dining Behavior for Designing Personalized Digital Menu

You are asked to participate in a research study conducted by Kwan Hong Lee, Ph.D. student from the Media Lab at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (M.I.T.). The results of this research will contribute to his Ph.D. dissertation. You were selected as a possible participant in this study because you are willing to try out digital menus in restaurants. You should read the information below, and ask questions about anything you do not understand, before deciding whether or not to participate.

PARTICIPATION AND WITHDRAWAL

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary and you are free to choose whether to be in it or not. If you choose to be in this study, you may subsequently withdraw from it at any time without penalty or consequences of any kind. The investigator may withdraw you from this research if circumstances arise which warrant doing so.

Your participation may be withdrawn if you try to exploit the computing resources in any way to intrude the privacy of another individual.

PURPOSE OF THE STUDY

The purpose of the study is to collect data to understand how people’s menu choices evolve for prediction and recommendation of menu items.

PROCEDURES

If you volunteer to participate in this study, we would ask you to do the following things:

  • Registration
    • Register to the DigitalMenu (http://menu.mit.edu/legals/) website, with your Facebook account.
    • During the trial you may optionally link Twitter (micro blogging site), Wesabe (online personal finance management) and TechCash (if from MIT) accounts. This will allow the system to assess for you how your financial information relates to your dining behavior when compared to others in similar demographics.
  • Download the free Digital Menu iPhone application from Apple App Store.
  • Restaurant Menu
    • When you arrive at the restaurant, notify the host that you are participating in the Digital Menu trial.
    • You will be given a table code by the waiter and an iPhone to order if you do not have your own.
    • You will use the phone application to order. You will log into the menu application, enter your user name and table code to order. After selecting the items, you will show them to the waiter to order.
  • Review
    • You will be able to rate what you ate on the web site.
    • Depending on the experiment, your choices will be shared with your friends and presented with your name. When shared with public, it will be anonymous.
  • Short Surveys
    • There will be a short survey (at most 5 questions) at the end of each month.
  • The duration of the study will last up to 6 months.

POTENTIAL RISKS AND DISCOMFORTS

If you do not feel comfortable sharing any of the purchases, you do not have to log them. You can always remove any data you log that you feel uncomfortable keeping in the server for research purposes. We ensure that it will be protected and purged after the trial. You will be able to view all that is logged from the DigitalMenu web site.

POTENTIAL BENEFITS

You will be able to experience mobile menu experience. You will be able to get recommendations.

PAYMENT FOR PARTICIPATION

  • The study is a real world behavioral economics study and as a result you will be paying for your own meal.
  • However, participants will be given 3 x $10 vouchers at Legal Sea Foods Kendall Square when they first visit and the first $10 voucher can be used right away towards purchase of an entree.
  • $10 Amazon gift certificate is rewarded for each order after participant rates, reviews and uploads the receipt on the menu website or $30 Amazon gift certificate is given if Wesabe account is linked (see FAQ).
  • $50 Legal Sea Food gift certificate will be awarded if 3 friends participate after one's participation.
  • A $200 Apple gift certificate will be raffled among all participants who have eaten at least once.
  • An iPad will be raffled among all participants who have visited 3 times.
  • Third timers will also be invited to a free meal to evaluate a new menu design.
  • * Receipts must be uploaded for rewards - photo upload via web or submitted in person.

CONFIDENTIALITY

No personally identifiable data will be shared with any outside party including Legal Sea Foods.

Any information that is obtained in connection with this study and that can be identified with you will remain confidential and will be disclosed only with your permission or as required by law.

All your data will be stored in standard ways web services store data. Any data collected by you will be available for review on the website when you login with your user account. All data collected from application usage will be linked only to coded identifiers for the participants, and not to any personal identifiers at any point during the capture process.

Any personal data that you contribute and are aggregated will only be used upon your consent. You will see visualizations of your data and aggregated data from your group or the public (all the participants) from your personal page. If at any time during the research trial you wish to withdraw, you may inform us and we will enable the ability for you to purge your data from your web page. After the analysis we will enable the ability to purge your data and withdraw from the system through the website. If you wish, you may continue to use the system after the research has ended. You will always have the option to purge all your data then.
